Steak

Description

  • steak
  • carrots
  • arugla
  • lemon
  • Texas toast

Steps

    Try this easy date night meal idea.

  1. Season a steak with salt and pepper
  2. Use your favorite method to cook your steak
  3. Slice into strips
  4. Drizzle carrots with olive oil
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Roast them in the oven
  7. Dress arugula with lemon juice and olive oil
  8. Layer the arugula and steak strips on slices of Texas toast and serve with carrots.
